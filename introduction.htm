INTRODUCTION OF LABORATORY

Spatial Image Processing Lab., its research interests mainly include in Optical Information Processing, Electron holography, Computing Holography, 3-D Image Display and Transmission, Spatial Filter, Evolution Image Processing, Intelligent Electron Detecting Circuit and so on. We have been trying to establish the Lab.

Our members are freely league group from the spatial image relative research fields in different university. They all are very actively supporting our research activities, and they all can freely express their advice, comments, and suggestion  in our seminars. We would like to invite other relative researchers to join our group to establish an intelligent international network research group for ourselves.

Our goal is to develop this spatial image technology in our country. And expect potentially to affect our students in our research fields.
